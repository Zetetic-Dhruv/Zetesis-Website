Zetesis Labs

Research group studying how discovery and inquiry itself can be engineered, and exploring the conclusions in practice.

Most groups treat not-knowing as a single fog to burn off with more data. We treat it as terrain with structure, crossed by two deliberate moves. Inquiry is the thinking that happens before the thinking: it turns what you cannot yet ask into a question. Discovery turns that question into something you can act on. Engineering both, so they hold under pressure and can be taught, is the work.
